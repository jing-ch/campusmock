# CampusMock — Form Field Mapping

## Intent
- intent: `What are you here for?`

## Shared Fields (appear in both Section 2 and Section 3)
- name: `What's your name?`
- college: `Which college are you in?`
- major: `What's your major?`
- enrollment: `When did you enroll? (e.g. 2024 Spring)`
- cultural_background_s2: `Cultural background — we use this for optional matching (e.g. Chinese, Brazilian, Indian)`
- cultural_background_s3: `Cultural background (optional, for matching)`
- availability_s2: `When are you generally free to interview others?`
- availability_s3: `When are you free to interview someone?`
- cv_s2: `Drop your CV here (PDF only, please 🙏)`
- cv_s3: `Drop your CV here (PDF only 🙏)`

## Section 2 Only (Requester)
- email_s2: `Your NEU email (we'll use this to reach you)`
- target_company: `What company are you interviewing for?`
- role: `What's the role? (e.g. SWE Intern, Data Analyst)`
- focus_area: `What kind of interview do you want to practice? (e.g. LeetCode, system design, behavioral)`
- slot_1: `Time slot 1 — when can you do the mock as a interviewee? (e.g. Mon Jan 20, 2pm PT)`
- slot_2: `Time slot 2 — when can you do the mock as a interviewee? (e.g. Mon Jan 20, 2pm PT)`
- slot_3: `Time slot 3 — when can you do the mock as a interviewee? (e.g. Mon Jan 20, 2pm PT)`
- pref_interviewer_bg: `Any preference on your interviewer's cultural background? (totally optional)`

## Section 3 Only (Interviewer-only)
- email_s3: `Your NEU email`
